# Quick_Weather

Simple gif viewer, primarily those from the [National Weather Service's](https://radar.gov/ridge/standard)
Just pulls the url of choice in the program, and plays it. Currently it just pulls per activation, and per hour. Also deletes them after an age of 7 days.

Will recommend [PS2EXE](https://github.com/MScholtes/PS2EXE) for this small application, with commands like -noConsole, -noError, and -exitOnCancel.
